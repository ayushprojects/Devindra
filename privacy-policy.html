Privacy Policy

Consent

Here at www.designasite101.com we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customer and keeping their data and information secure. The company only uses the information collected by customers lawfully and in accordance with the current Data Protection and legislation. When using the website customers give their consent for their personal information to be used by the website in the ways that are outlined below in this privacy policy.

This Privacy Policy adheres to the Data Protection Act and is GDPR Compliant.

Company owning URL

The URL of this website www.designasite101.com is wholly owned by the owner of the company TOACAK LIMITED. Their office is based at 100 Edenbridge Road, Birmingham, United Kingdom, B28 8PR.

Personal Data Collected And How it is Used

When using the website and when setting up an account in order to purchase electronic goods and services from www.designasite.com the following data may be collected:

· Identity Data

This includes information such as: title, first name and last name

· Contact Data

This includes information such as: email address, billing address, delivery address, location, country, telephone number

· Transaction Data This includes information such as: details of your purchases and the fulfilment of your orders (such as basket contents, order number, number of items, product details, discounts, shipping costs etc), discount codes redeemed, payments to and from you, correspondence or communication with you in respect of your orders.

· Technical Data

This includes information such as: details of device(s) you use to access our website, your internet protocol (IP) address, login data, your username, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, information collected through cookies, pixel tags and other technologies

· Profile Data

This includes information such as: purchases or orders made by you, products purchased, feedback, preferences

· Usage Data

This includes information such as: how and when you use our website, how you moved around it, what you searched for, website performance statistics, weblogs and other communication data.

· Marketing Data

This includes information such as: whether you have opted in to receive marketing from us, date of opt in/unsubscribed

Purpose For Collecting Data

The data that is collected may be used by the company in the following ways outlined below. The company will only use customer personal data for these reasons outlined below, unless the company believes that it reasonably considers that it needs to use the data in another was in which case the company will contact the customer before doing so and fully explain the purpose as well as any legal basis which allows it to use the data in another way.

Data is used for the following purposes:

· To create an account with www.designasite101.com and register a new customer.

· To process and deliver a customers order including: recording order details, keeping the customer informed about the order status, in order to process payments and refunds, in order to assist fraud prevention and detection and to provide delivery information and notifications of downloads.

· To build a profile of the customer including: offering personalised product and service recommendations, perform targeted marketing and for the purpose of fraud protection.

· To manage our relationship with the customer, including: providing the customer with information, products and services that the customer requests from us. In order to notify the customer about changes to our Services, Terms and Conditions or Privacy Policy.

· To use data analytics to improve our website, products and services, marketing, customer relationships and experiences as well as for market research, statistical purposes.

· To recommend products, service discounts and offers that may be of interest to the customer, including to send the customer such information by email. These emails can be opted out of by clicking on the link available at the bottom of all emails.

· As part of the company’s efforts to keep our site safe and secure

Rights that users have about their data

Customers have the following rights regarding their data in accordance with the correct terms of current legislation:

- To require information as to whether personal data is retained as well as the right to access and see the personal data that is retained

- To request to remove or restrict the use of a customer’s personal data if it is incomplete, inaccurate or no longer needed

- To withdraw consent to processing of personal data at any time

- To take legal action regarding any potential breach of a customer’s rights

- To receive a copy of the personal data that is stored

- To not be the subject of any automated decision making or automated email marketing

Cookie Policy

As a customer interacts with the website, we may collect information on them in the form of cookies. Cookies are small files of information that are stored on the customer’s computer and help us as a company to provide a tailored browsing experience.

Customers may opt out of using cookies by changing the information on their browser setting.

Security and Fraud

At www.designasite101.com your privacy and security is very important to us. The company has appropriate security measures in place in order to protect customers against loss, misuse and any alteration of personal data which is under the company’s control.

Our security and privacy policies are constantly reviewed so that only authorised personnel have access to your personal data.
